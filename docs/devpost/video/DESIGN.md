# LyraShield AI Build Week video design reference

## Overview

The video uses the real LyraShield AI marketing and dashboard interfaces as the primary visual evidence. HyperFrames only supplies a brief opener, a brief closer, restrained transitions, and captions; it must not recreate product screens or imply product behavior that the recording does not show.

## Colors

- **Canvas:** `#0a0c0e`
- **Raised surface:** `#0e1a28`
- **Primary text:** `#edf8fb`
- **Muted text:** `#91a7b8`
- **Accent:** `#54d6df`
- **Accent dim:** `#153d48`
- **Caution:** `#f3b95f`

## Typography

- **Display and captions:** Bricolage Grotesque Variable, 600–800.
- **Technical labels:** JetBrains Mono Variable, 600.
- **Supporting copy:** Inter Variable, 500–600.

## Motion

- Calm, decisive, and evidence-led: use one focus pull for the opening, 300–400 ms push transitions between related product states, and a gentle fade at the close.
- Keep live browser footage at normal speed except clearly labeled accelerated waiting time.
- Use no simulated terminal, dashboard, score, finding, or scan UI.

## Do not

- Do not use third-party logos, stock footage, or copyrighted music.
- Do not use hype effects, glitch transitions, or motion that makes the evidence feel fabricated.
- Do not obscure limitations, synthetic-data labels, or public-product boundaries.
