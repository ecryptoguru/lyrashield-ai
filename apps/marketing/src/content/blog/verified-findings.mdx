In security, a noisy finding is worse than no finding. The team that handles it has to triage, reproduce, and then decide. LyraShield AI flips that: every finding reaches you with the evidence attached.

## What verification means

A verified finding is reproducible. It has a trigger, a consequence, and a recommended fix. It is not a guess from a pattern.
