In security, a noisy finding creates work, but hiding uncertain findings creates false confidence. LyraShield AI keeps detected candidates visible while preserving whether independent verification exists.

## What verification means

A verified finding requires an independent verification receipt. Detection confidence helps triage; it does not become proof. A deterministic clean retest can validate a fix only when that scanner had complete relevant coverage, while incomplete evidence remains inconclusive.
